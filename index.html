Welcome to War Thunder, a free to play MMO game incorporating planes, armored vehicles, naval vessels and high-tech military equipment in a realistic environment. War Thunder was selected as the best simulation game at Gamescom 2013 Awards. It won best game, best developer, best technology and best sound at the KRI 2013 awards. With over 70 million registered players on platforms ranging from PC to Playstation and Xbox, War Thunder was one of the most played games on Steam in 2019.
