War Thunder includes four different armed forces from ten countries to compete in 3 game modes.

Aviation Ground Forces Naval Forces
  • Two teams with planes from different nations fight to complete thier objective, such as destroying targets or capturing a point
  • Damage models and physics are simplified to make the game easier
  • Teams are formed with no regards to nationality and are tasked to either capture points or destroy enemy tanks
  • Tank handling is simplified - they move and turn faster than their real counterparts
  • There are two sections in the Naval Forces:
    • Bluewater Fleet - Large ships used for combat out in the open ocean
    • Coastal Fleet - Smaller and faster boats
  • Ships are stronger and more maneuverable than in real life
