War Thunder History

2011

Gaijin Entertainment announces the release of a new game called "War Thunder" in closed beta testing

2012

Second "Ground Forces" game mode is released

2013

War Thunder reaches 3,000,000 players, recives "Best Simulation Game" at Gamescom, and is launched on multiple platforms

2014

More features are added to the game including X-ray mode and tanks from different nations

2015

Increased game physics and graphics with the new Dagor 4.0 game engine

2016

War Thunder is officially released with over 500 aircraft and 300 tanks!

2023

War thunder has over 50,000 simultaneous live players!
